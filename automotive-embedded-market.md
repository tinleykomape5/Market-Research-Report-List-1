<p><p>The automotive embedded market is divided into two primary types: embedded hardware and embedded software. Embedded hardware includes components like microcontrollers, sensors, and processors that enable vehicle functionalities such as safety, navigation, and infotainment. On the other hand, embedded software comprises the programs and operating systems that control these hardware components, facilitating tasks like communication, data processing, and vehicle diagnostics. Together, they enhance vehicle performance, efficiency, and user experience in modern automotive systems.</p></p>
