<p><strong>The Hot Runner for Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Gate</li><li>Valve Gate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot runners in the packaging market use advanced injection molding technology to enhance efficiency and product quality. Open gate systems allow the molten material to flow freely into the mold cavity, ideal for simple designs and faster cycles. Conversely, valve gate systems utilize a mechanical valve to control the flow, enabling precision filling and reducing waste, making them suitable for complex geometries. Both types improve production rate, minimize scrap, and enhance consistency in packaging applications.</p></p>
