<p><ul><li>Fixed Port</li><li>Modular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise routers are categorized into two main market types: fixed port and modular. Fixed port routers have a set number of built-in ports, offering simplicity and ease of deployment for smaller networks. They are typically cost-effective and ideal for organizations with straightforward connectivity needs. Conversely, modular routers provide flexibility through interchangeable modules, allowing businesses to customize and expand their network capabilities as they grow. This adaptability makes them suitable for larger enterprises with complex networking demands.</p></p>
