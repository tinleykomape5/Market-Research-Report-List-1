<p><p>The UV cured resin market comprises several key components: oligomers, monomers, photoinitiators, and additives. Oligomers are long-chain molecules that provide the resin's backbone, enhancing flexibility and durability. Monomers are smaller molecules that react with oligomers to create a solid structure upon curing. Photoinitiators trigger the polymerization process when exposed to UV light. Additives are used to modify properties such as viscosity, color, and performance characteristics. Together, these elements enable the production of high-performance UV cured resins for various applications.</p></p>
